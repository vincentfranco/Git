CACI Non-Surgical Facelifting

Experience the latest in non-surgical facial toning with our CACI treatments. Utilizing state-of-the-art technology, CACI helps to lift and tone facial muscles, reducing the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles. CACI treatment systems deliver visible results without surgery or invasive procedures. With over 20 years of success, these treatments have garnered significant demand and celebrity endorsements. Each session begins with a consultation to tailor the treatment to your needs.

Non-surgical treatments are renowned for their use of microcurrent technology to tone, lift, and rejuvenate facial and body muscles, providing a non-invasive alternative to cosmetic surgery. Here are some of the most popular CACI treatments:

  • Facelift
  • Eye Lift
  • Jowl Lift
  • Hydratone
  • Wrinkle
  • Acne
  • Lip
  • Scarring

CACI Non-Surgical Facelift

Description: This signature CACI treatment uses microcurrent impulses to lift and tone facial muscles, while also improving skin elasticity and reducing fine lines. It’s often called the "non-surgical facelift" and provides a natural lift.

Benefits: Firming and toning of facial muscles, improvement of facial contours, reduction in the appearance of fine lines and wrinkles.

Ideal For: Clients looking to achieve a more youthful appearance without invasive procedures.

CACI Eye Lift

Description: This treatment targets the delicate eye area, using microcurrent to lift and tone the muscles around the eyes, while also reducing puffiness and dark circles.

Benefits: Lifts and firms muscles around the eyes, reduces eye bags and dark circles, smooths fine lines in the eye area.

Ideal For: Clients with tired, aging eyes looking for a refreshed appearance.

CACI Jowl Lift

Description: The CACI Jowl Lift specifically targets sagging jowls, a common area of concern as we age. It uses microcurrent to lift and firm the jawline.

Benefits: Lifts and defines the jawline, reduces the appearance of sagging around the jowls.

Ideal For: Clients wanting a more contoured jawline and neck area.

CACI Hydratone Facial

Description: This hydrating treatment combines microcurrent with a specialized mask infused with collagen, hyaluronic acid, and rose water to deeply hydrate, firm, and plump the skin.

Benefits: Intense hydration, plumping effect, and improved skin elasticity.

Ideal For: Clients with dry or dehydrated skin, or anyone wanting a quick boost of radiance.

CACI Wrinkle Revolution

Description: This treatment specifically targets deep lines and wrinkles using CACI’s Amino Lift Peptide Complex alongside wrinkle-smoothing microcurrent. It’s particularly effective for frown lines and crow’s feet.

Benefits: Softening of deep lines, smoother and more youthful-looking skin.

Ideal For: Clients concerned with specific lines and wrinkles, such as forehead lines or smile lines.

CACI Acne Treatment

Description: This treatment uses LED light therapy along with microcurrent to reduce acne-causing bacteria, improve skin texture, and calm inflammation.

Benefits: Reduces acne and inflammation, promotes healing, and soothes the skin.

Ideal For: Clients with acne-prone or blemish-prone skin.

CACI Lip Treatment (CACI Lip Booster)

Description: The Lip Booster treatment focuses on enhancing lip volume and definition without the need for fillers. It uses a combination of microcurrent and a specialized Amino Lift Peptide Complex.

Benefits: Fuller, more defined lips with a natural look.

Ideal For: Clients looking for subtle lip enhancement without injections.

CACI Scarring and Stretch Mark Treatment

Description: This treatment uses LED light therapy and microcurrent technology to improve the appearance of scarring and stretch marks, promoting skin healing and regeneration.

Benefits: Reduces the appearance of scars and stretch marks, improves skin texture.

Ideal For: Clients with acne scars, surgical scars, or stretch marks seeking a smoother skin surface.
