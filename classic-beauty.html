Our Treatments at Beauty with Christina

  • Waxing
  • Brows

Facials

Customised facial treatments for all skin types, focusing on rejuvenation, hydration, and radiance. Our expert estheticians will tailor each facial to your unique skin concerns, ensuring optimal results.

Experience the ultimate glow with our personalized facials. Revitalize your skin and walk out feeling refreshed and radiant. Book your appointment today and let your natural beauty shine through!

Melt away stress and tension with our soothing massages. Rejuvenate your body and mind. Schedule your session now and step into a world of relaxation and tranquility!
